# Context
James Altucher shows readers how they can succeed despite their flaws, not because of a lack of flaws. This is hugely refreshing in a world of rah-rah positive thinking gurus woh are all forced-smiles. 

# Content 

If you're reading this, you may want to reinvent yourself.

For Altucher, bankrupt, divorced and unable to publish a book, he was down on his luck. Deeply unhappy, he realized he had been living like an amateur. To really life an authentic and virtuous life, you should know:

- Health is the most important thing of all.
- Have humility and learn from others always.
- Be grateful for the many lessons you get
- Only be around people you love and who inspire you.
- Don't waste this fleeting life doing things to follow the status quo.
- Creativity is a muscle. Daily, be creative.
- Life will always mar you. Do not forget its lessons.
- Giving 80% is good enough. Mistakes are normal

While it is entirely possible to find a summary of this book by [Altucher himself](https://archive.jamesaltucher.com/blog/financial-fridays-today-reinvent-yourself/), this summary is a deep dive into all 47 chapters, which themselves are summaries of his podcasts.
